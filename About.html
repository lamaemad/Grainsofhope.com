What is the Grains of Hope foundation?

Grains of Hope is a non-profit charity foundation started in early 2020. As it can be observed, many countries around the globe are experiencing economic turmoil; and so, Grains of Hope took it upon themselves to help those who cannot afford their basic nessecities. Our team is made up of more than 3000 people, actively recruiting more members in order to further our influence. As of 2023, we were capable of aiding more than 30,000 individuals across the world's nations, and we hope to help more.
